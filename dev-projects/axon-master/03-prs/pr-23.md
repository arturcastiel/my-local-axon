- `helpers/cd-gap-c1-p3-goals-extracted.md` → G.doc.01-03; G.wf.01 (canonical-flows doc).

## Design notes
- Three new files, Diátaxis-aligned (reference for `STUDY` and `PLAN`; how-to for `WORKFLOWS`):
  - `workspace/AXON-DOCS-WORKFLOWS.md` — 8 named workflows (WF1..WF8) with step-by-step, drawn from `cd-wf-c1-p1-canonical-flows.md`.

  - `workspace/AXON-DOCS-PLAN.md` — 4 implemented modes + future 6 noted as Post-1.0.
